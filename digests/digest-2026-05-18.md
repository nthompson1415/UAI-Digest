This agreement, part of a broader legislative package, also introduces new rules for AI-generated intimate content. The extended deadlines provide companies with additional time for risk classification, governance framework development, and technical documentation preparation.
[Latham & Watkins](https://www.lw.com/thoughtLeadership/eu-ai-act-omnibus-agreement-amendments-deadlines) · 2026-05-13

Illinois Senate Democrats have introduced a package of eight bills aimed at regulating various aspects of artificial intelligence, including consumer protection, chatbot transparency, and AI use in schools. The move is partly motivated by a perceived lack of federal action on AI policy, with the aim of establishing a de facto national standard by aligning with legislation in California and New York.
